<h2>📌 Project Overview</h2>

<p>
This project analyzes customer financial behavior to assess credit risk and optimize lending decisions. 
Using Python-based EDA and Power BI dashboards, the project identifies risk patterns across income segments, 
deposit behavior, and credit utilization.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>📸 Dashboard Preview</h2>

<p>
<b>Dashboard Objective:</b> Quantify customer risk by analyzing loan exposure, deposit strength, and engagement behavior to support safer lending decisions.
</p>

<h2>💡 Overall Insights</h2>

<ul>
<li>Loan-to-deposit imbalance is the strongest indicator of credit risk</li>
<li>Customer engagement duration inversely correlates with default risk</li>
<li>High-income segments drive majority of revenue but require monitoring for high exposure</li>
<li>~20–30% of customers contribute disproportionately to risk exposure</li>
</ul>
